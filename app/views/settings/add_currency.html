Update accepted currencies (For all kickmarket sellers)

Changing this setting will allow you to accept payments from other currencies which will then be converted to your normal currency.
  1. Log in to your PayPal account at https://www.paypal.com.

  2. Click on your profile icon

    <%= image_tag('add_currency_1.png', class: "fixed-ratio-resize") %>

  3. Click ‘My selling tools’, then click ‘Update’ under Credit card statement name. 4. Change top setting to ‘Yes, accept…’. Then click ‘Save’.

    <%= image_tag('add_currency_2.png', class: "fixed-ratio-resize") %>

  4. Change top setting to ‘Yes, accept…’. Then click ‘Save’.
